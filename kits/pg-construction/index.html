Decorator's notes — how we'd run your kit

The thinking behind every choice — matched to your brand, your garments and the field.

Method matched to the garmentEmbroidery on the office layer — polos, softshells and jackets — for a premium, hard-wearing finish; screen print on the hi-vis and tees, where it's brighter and more cost-effective at volume.
Colour chosen for impact & valueYour mark is a single brand colour (#1D3774), so a clean 1-colour print reproduces it perfectly and keeps your per-piece cost down. On hi-vis it prints white with the garment showing through the mark — a sharp, on-brand look at the best price.
Every mark reads on every colourInks and threads are matched to each garment — full brand colour where it pops, reversed to white where a dark mark would otherwise vanish. Your logo is never lost on the fabric.
Placed to spec, safe by designEvery mark is sized and positioned to the manufacturer's decoration spec and kept clear of the reflective tape — so the garment's hi-vis safety rating is never compromised.
One consistent lookThe same brand treatment across office and field, so your crew looks unified from the boardroom polo to the jobsite vest.
