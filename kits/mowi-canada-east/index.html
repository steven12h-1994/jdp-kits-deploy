Why a branded program for Mowi

Your crews are Mowi, on every wharf

Mowi Canada East runs two distinct wardrobes off one brand kit — the field and marine crews who keep the salmon sites running in Atlantic weather, and the site-management team who represent Mowi in the office, in audits and in front of partners. A sharp, consistent program serves both.

01

Look the part, win the trust

A uniformed crew reads as a serious, safety-first operator — to partners, auditors and the coastal communities Mowi works alongside.

02

A billboard on every wharf

Branded crews on boats, feed barges and at the shore base put the Mowi name in front of every community along the coast — visibility that works every shift.

03

Gear crews wear in Atlantic weather

Kit that performs in NL wind, rain and cold gets worn on the clock and off. Issued gear that works signals Mowi invests in its people.

04

Seen and compliant on the water

The field layer carries the Mowi mark and keeps crews CSA-visible in low light and on deck — safety and brand in one garment.

Our recommendation for Mowi

Run a two-layer program: a client-facing layer (quilted vest, softshell & performance polo) for site management and the office, and a field layer (traffic tee, tear-away vest & hi-vis hoodie) that keeps every crew CSA-compliant and on-brand. One look — feed barge to boardroom.

Specifications & logo placement

Your brand, applied with care

Mowi Coral#FF6F4A · accent
Charcoal#2C2A29 · primary ink
White#FFFFFF · reverse
  • Embroidery is stitched full-colour on the office layer — white reverse on the dark garments so the mark always reads.
  • Screen print on hi-vis comes 1-colour (charcoal, recommended) or 2-colour (charcoal + a white keyline that lifts the mark off the orange).
  • Ink is matched to contrast on every garment — charcoal on hi-vis, white on black/navy — so the logo never disappears into the fabric.
